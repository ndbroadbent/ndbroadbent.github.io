Weather Notifier

Nathan Broadbent

Mar 20, 2011 — 1 min read

This is just a little idea I had on the bus.

Outside my apartment, I have a bucket for umbrellas.

A program checks the local weather on weather.com from time to time. If there's any chance of rain, it switches on the lights installed in the bucket.

So when I walk out the door, I know whether or not I should take an umbrella to work.
